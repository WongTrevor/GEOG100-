Narrative

Victoria Harbour

Where maritime commerce and pollution meet marine ecosystems. Efforts focus on reducing maritime pollution and understanding industrial impacts on biodiversity.

Tai Mo Shan

High-altitude habitats showcase resilience. Observations highlight how species adapt amidst climate variability and urban encroachment.

Mai Po Reserve

A critical wetland haven for migratory birds. Conservation emphasizes maintaining crucial habitats along avian flyways.

Interactive Map

Explore pivotal locations and gain a spatial perspective on how these areas are interconnected, shaping both environmental conditions and urban life.

Highlights

Victoria Harbour

Examines urban pollution, maritime traffic, and the health of marine species. Highlights the push for cleaner, sustainable maritime practices.

Tai Mo Shan

Studies high-altitude biodiversity facing evolving climates. A testament to nature’s adaptability amid environmental stressors.

Mai Po Reserve

Focuses on wetlands and migratory birds. Underscores wetlands as key habitats requiring vigilant protection and careful management.

Lantau Island

Addresses sustainable development in resource-rich but delicate ecosystems. Balances tourism, infrastructure, and habitat protection.

Hong Kong Park

Shows the importance of green urban spaces for mental well-being, biodiversity support, and improved city resilience.
